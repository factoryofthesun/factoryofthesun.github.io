I'm creating this website at a strange and uncertain time in my life, so most likely any attempt to summarize "me" will most likely be wildly inaccurate in a year or two. Nevertheless, here is the "me" as I believe it to currently exist.

Professionally, I am currently a predoc fellow at the Tobin Center for Economic Policy, working under John Eric Humphries and Yusuke Narita. I am not very interested, however, in traditional economics that deals with modeling market equilibria under various constraints. At this point I'm more interested in engaging with economic-political-social life more directly, in the form of real-time policy evaluation, aiding in the development of public interest technology, and curtailing algorithmic bias. My personal projects will center around these themes.

Intellectually, I'm a lazy dilettante. I can engage in almost anything as long as I believe it to be a "hard" problem, though I can just as easily disengage once I am satisfied with my level of "essential understanding". Some hobbies/interests I take more seriously are volleyball, Marxism (Platypus Affiliated Society), politics, and anime/manga.

Some other categories that I will likely eventually post about: aesthetic experiences, film, theories of intelligence/consciousness, video games, podcasts.

This website is intended to present my "personal brand" in a more serious intellectual fashion. For my aesthetic tastes and shitposting, please refer to my instagram @guan.dick and twitter @ccummm. Both seldom updated, but this might change once I stabilize my sense of self.
